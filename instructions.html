How to Play the Memory Card Game

  • ✅ Click on a card to reveal its hidden symbol.
  • ✅ Find and click another card to try and match the first one.
  • ✅ If the two cards match, they stay flipped.
  • ✅ If they don't match, they flip back over after a short delay.
  • ✅ Keep flipping cards and matching pairs until all cards are revealed.
  • ✅ Try to complete the game in the shortest time and fewest moves!
